About Us

History

Originating from the Galapagos Islands, we are the last vestiges of a small local tribe.
In 1874, the colonists invaded us and decided to found a circus in front of our sense of the show (and especially because they found us too cute). Since then, we travel the world with our troop to share with all our numbers, and make the pleasure of young and old..

Troop

Jugglers: Triplet brothers who work in total symbiosis, they haven't equal in the world of juggling. Swords with fiery spears... Nothing is too dangerous for them when it comes to marveling at you.
Trapezists: A great sexy and a little fat for an unusual duo. Their jumps will delight you and make you shudder with fear
Animals: A couple of tigers, giraphes, ostriches, but we don't have unicorns sorry ...
Mr Loyal: The leader of the troop.
